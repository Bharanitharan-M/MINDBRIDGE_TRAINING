Gallery: Memorable Moments of Virat Kohli

U19 Winning Moments

In 2008, Virat Kohli led the Indian U19 cricket team to a spectacular victory in the ICC U19 World Cup held in Malaysia. Under his captaincy, the team displayed remarkable grit and determination, with Virat himself contributing significantly with both bat and leadership.

First International Century

On December 24, 2009, Virat Kohli etched his name into cricketing history by scoring his first international century during an ODI match against Sri Lanka in Kolkata. Chasing a formidable target of 316, Virat's composed knock of 107 runs off 114 balls was instrumental in India's successful chase.

First Test International Century

On January 15, 2012, Virat Kohli achieved a significant milestone in his career by scoring his first Test century during the fourth Test of India’s tour of Australia in Adelaide. Battling a formidable Australian bowling attack, Virat displayed immense grit and determination, scoring a brilliant 116 runs off 213 balls.

First Test International Century

On September 8, 2022, Virat Kohli delighted cricket fans worldwide by scoring his first-ever T20I century during the Asia Cup match against Afghanistan in Dubai. Virat’s unbeaten knock of 122 runs off just 61 balls was a masterclass in precision, power, and elegance.
